THE SUNNAHS OF MARRIAGE


And among His signs is this, that He created for you Mates from among yourselves,
so that you may Dwell in Peace and Tranquility with them, and He has put Love and Mercy between your Hearts ♥


NUMBER 1

It is Sunnah to Marry Older Women.



NUMBER 2

It is Sunnah to Marry a Divorcee.



NUMBER 3

It is Sunnah to Marry a Widow.



NUMBER 4

It is Sunnah to help Women in Household Chores
i.e. Cooking, Cleaning, Washing etc.



NUMBER 5

It is Sunnah to put food with your hand in your
Wife's Mouth as an Expression of Love.
(Graded as a form of charity)



NUMBER 6

It is Sunnah to verbally
Express Love, Appreciation &
Respect to your Wife.



NUMBER 7

It is Sunnah to Forgive his/her Mistakes.



NUMBER 8

It is Sunnah to keep yourself
Looking Pleasant for your Husband/Wife.

NUMBER 9

It is Sunnah to know the Feelings of
Your Husband/Wife and Console him/her when needed.



NUMBER 10

It is Sunnah to be playful with your Husband/Wife &
Spend Quality time having fun together.
(Racing, Story-Telling, Sharing Happy Occasions are some best examples)



NUMBER 11

It is Sunnah to
Recline on your Husband/Wife's Lap.



NUMBER 12

It is Sunnah to not
Dislose his/her Private matters to
other Family members or Friends.



NUMBER 13

It is Sunnah to
Love and Respect his/her Parents.



NUMBER 14

It is Sunnah to
call your Wife with Beautiful Names.





Put your Trust in Allah
May Allah SWT bless all the
Marriages with Happiness, Ameen.




Marriage is part of my sunnah, and whoever does not follow my sunnah has nothing to do with me,
Whoever has the means, let him get married, and whoever does not, then he should fast for it will diminish his desire!

- Prophet Muhammad S.A.W
